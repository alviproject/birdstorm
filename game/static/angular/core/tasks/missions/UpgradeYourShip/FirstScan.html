Go to System 1 and select one of terrestrial planets, for example this one.

Then choose "Resources" tab in the details panel (it's next to "Planet" tab which is select by default).

Great! Now click "Scan" at the right of "Resources" tab.

This operation will allow you to find out what kind of resources are located on the planet.

Well done!

Scanning operation takes some time, depending on planet type, deepness and your equipped scanner.

Having a better scanner will also allow you to scan deeper and find more valuable resources, but we will get to this later.
