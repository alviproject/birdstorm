About BirdStorm - browser based, MMO game in space

You start as a pilot of a small ship, but if you are clever enough you can quickly impact result of inter-galactic war.

Spaceships

Your ship can be upgraded, customized and enhanced to meet your own requirements. But be aware, it can be also damaged, broken or even destroyed.

Spaceship types:

Raven

Basic, but quite solid and very flexible ship. Affordable price and availability of spare parts and upgrades make it one of most popular vehicle in this part of the galaxy.

It's not fancy, but if you are not the type who cares you won't be disappointed!
